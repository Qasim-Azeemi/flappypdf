Are my images safe and private?

▼

Yes. FlappyPDF processes everything in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device — there's no server, no upload, no storage of any kind.

What image formats are supported?

▼

JPG, PNG, and WEBP are supported. WEBP images are automatically converted to JPEG internally before embedding, so they work perfectly in any PDF viewer.

How many images can I add?

▼

There's no set limit on image count. We recommend keeping the total file size under 150MB for smooth performance on most devices. Very large batches may take a few seconds to process.
